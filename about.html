Our Journey

Sharp Colours was founded in 2012 by Satya Prakash Verma with a simple vision: to transform Pune's buildings with quality painting that lasts. What started as a small painting service has grown into one of Pune's most trusted painting contractors.

From painting single rooms to entire building complexes, we've built our reputation on precision, quality materials, and exceptional customer service. Our journey reflects our commitment to making every space more beautiful and durable.

Our Mission

To deliver flawless painting solutions that enhance spaces, protect properties, and exceed customer expectations through skilled craftsmanship and premium materials.

Our Vision

To become Pune's most preferred painting service provider, recognized for innovation, reliability, and transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments.

Our Painting Process

Step-by-step approach to ensure flawless results every time

1

Consultation

We visit your site, understand your requirements, discuss color options, and provide a detailed proposal with timeline and cost.

2

Surface Preparation

Proper preparation is key. We clean, repair cracks, level surfaces, and apply primers to ensure perfect paint adhesion.

3

Color Selection

We help you choose the right colors and finishes based on lighting, space usage, and your personal preferences.

4

Painting Execution

Our skilled painters apply paint with precision using appropriate techniques for walls, ceilings, trims, and exteriors.

5

Quality Check

Every area is inspected for consistency, coverage, and finish. Touch-ups are done as needed to achieve perfection.

6

Cleanup & Handover

We clean the work area thoroughly, remove all waste materials, and hand over your beautifully transformed space.
